## Payment Method Types {#types}

### 1. Credit & Debit Cards
#### Supported Networks
- **Visa** (All regions)
- **Mastercard** (All regions)
- **American Express** (All regions)
- **Discover** (US, Canada, China)
- **UnionPay** (China, Asia)
- **JCB** (Japan, Asia)

#### Setup Requirements
- PCI DSS compliance (handled by Loft)
- Basic business verification
- No additional accounts needed

### 2. Digital Wallets

#### Supported Wallets
| Wallet | Regions | Best For |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **Apple Pay** | 60+ countries | iOS users, in-person payments |
| **Google Pay** | 40+ countries | Android users, online checkout |
| **Alipay** | China, Asia | Chinese attendees |
| **WeChat Pay** | China, Asia | Chinese attendees |
| **GrabPay** | Southeast Asia | Mobile-first users |
| **Sofort** | Europe | Bank transfers in EU |

#### Setup Process
1. Enable in payment settings
2. Complete additional verification (if required)
3. Test transactions

### 3. Bank Transfers & Direct Debit

#### Available Methods
- **ACH** (US, Canada)
- **SEPA** (EU/EEA)
- **BACS** (UK)
- **PIX** (Brazil)
- **iDEAL** (Netherlands)
- **Boleto** (Brazil)
- **OXXO** (Mexico)

#### Setup Requirements
- Verified business account
- Local bank account (in most cases)
- Additional verification documents

## Regional Availability {#regional}

### North America
- **Primary**: Cards, ACH, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Alternatives**: PayPal, Venmo (US)
- **Processing**: 1-3 business days

### Europe
- **Primary**: Cards, SEPA, Sofort, iDEAL
- **Alternatives**: Giropay, Bancontact
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days (SEPA)

### Asia Pacific
- **Primary**: Cards, Alipay, WeChat Pay, GrabPay
- **Alternatives**: PayNow (Singapore), PayID (Australia)
- **Processing**: 1-3 business days

### Latin America
- **Primary**: Cards, PIX, OXXO, Boleto
- **Alternatives**: Mercado Pago
- **Processing**: 3-5 business days (bank transfers)

## Setup & Configuration {#setup}

### Enabling Payment Methods
1. Go to **Finances** > **Payment Methods**
2. Toggle on desired methods
3. Configure country restrictions
4. Set default methods
5. Save changes

### Method-Specific Settings

#### Credit/Debit Cards
- Enable/disable card types
- Set up 3D Secure
- Configure AVS checks
- Set transaction limits

#### Digital Wallets
- Upload button assets
- Configure display options
- Set up automatic detection

#### Bank Transfers
- Upload company logo
- Set payment instructions
- Configure expiration period
- Enable automatic reminders

## Fees & Processing Times {#fees}

### Transaction Fees
| Method | Standard Fee | International Fee | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|-------|
| Credit/Debit | 2.9% + $0.30 | 3.9% + $0.30 | Lower for non-profits |
| Digital Wallets | 2.5% + $0.30 | 3.5% + $0.30 | Varies by region |
| Bank Transfers | 1% ($1 max) | 2% ($2 max) | Higher for some regions |
| Alternative | 3-5% | Varies | Depends on method |

### Processing Times
- **Cards**: 2-3 business days
- **Digital Wallets**: 1-2 business days
- **Bank Transfers**: 3-5 business days
- **Alternative Methods**: Varies (1-7 days)

## Troubleshooting {#troubleshooting}

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Payment Declined
1. **Card Issues**
   - Check for typos
   - Verify sufficient funds
   - Contact card issuer

2. **Authentication Failed**
   - Complete 3D Secure
   - Try different browser/device
   - Contact bank for verification

#### Missing Payouts
1. **First Payout**
   - 7-10 day holding period
   - Complete verification
   - Check for verification emails

2. **Ongoing Payouts**
   - Check payout schedule
   - Verify bank details
   - Look for processing delays
