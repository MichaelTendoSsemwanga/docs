# Payment Methods & Processing

Configure and manage payment options for your events with Loft's secure payment processing.

## Supported Payment Methods

### Credit/Debit Cards
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- JCB
- UnionPay

### Digital Wallets
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Alipay
- WeChat Pay

### Bank Transfers
- ACH (US)
- SEPA (EU)
- BACS (UK)
- Direct Debit

## Setting Up Payment Methods

### For Event Organizers
1. Go to **Finances** > **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Select your preferred payment processor
4. Complete the verification process
5. Set as default (optional)

### For Attendees
1. During checkout, select **Add Payment Method**
2. Choose your preferred payment type
3. Enter payment details
4. Save for future purchases (optional)

## Payment Processing

### Transaction Flow
1. Attendee completes purchase
2. Payment is authorized
3. Funds are held securely
4. Payout is processed according to your schedule

### Processing Fees
| Payment Method | Fee | Processing Time |
|---------------|-----|----------------|
| Credit Cards | 2.9% + $0.30 | 2-3 business days |
| Digital Wallets | 2.5% + $0.30 | 1-2 business days |
| Bank Transfers | 1% (max $10) | 3-5 business days |

## Security & Compliance

### PCI Compliance
- All payments are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
- Credit card data is never stored on our servers
- Tokenization for secure recurring payments

### Fraud Prevention
- Advanced fraud detection
- 3D Secure authentication
- Address verification (AVS)

## Managing Payouts

### Payout Schedule
- **Standard**: Weekly payouts (Tuesdays)
- **Express**: Next business day (additional 1% fee)
- **Custom**: Set your own schedule

### Payout Methods
- Direct to bank account (recommended)
- Paper check (additional fees apply)
- PayPal (for international payouts)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Payment declined | Verify card details, check with bank |
| Missing payout | Check processing schedule, verify bank details |
| Refund issues | Contact support for assistance |

## Best Practices

### For Event Organizers
- Offer multiple payment options
- Be transparent about fees
- Set clear refund policies
- Monitor transactions regularly

### For Attendees
- Use saved payment methods for faster checkout
- Keep payment information up to date
- Review receipts and confirmations
