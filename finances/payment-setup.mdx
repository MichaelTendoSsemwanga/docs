# Payment Processing Setup

Configure your payment processing to start accepting payments for your events.

## Supported Payment Methods

### Credit/Debit Cards
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

### Digital Wallets
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- PayPal

## Setting Up Your Payment Account

### Step 1: Access Payment Settings
1. Go to **Organization Settings** > **Payments**
2. Click **Set Up Payments**
3. Select your country/region

### Step 2: Business Information
- Legal business name
- Business address
- Tax ID/EIN (if applicable)
- Business type (Individual/Company/Non-profit)

### Step 3: Bank Account
- Account holder name
- Routing number
- Account number
- Account type (Checking/Savings)

### Step 4: Identity Verification
- Upload government-issued ID
- Provide personal information
- Complete verification process

## Payout Schedule

### Standard Payouts
- First payout: 7-10 business days after first successful payment
- Subsequent payouts: 2 business days after the payment is processed

### Payout Thresholds
- Minimum payout: $10.00
- Maximum per transaction: $50,000.00

## Fees & Pricing

### Processing Fees
- Credit/Debit cards: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- International cards: 3.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- ACH transfers: 1% ($1 maximum fee)

### No Hidden Fees
- No monthly fees
- No setup fees
- No cancellation fees

## Security & Compliance

### PCI Compliance
- All transactions are PCI-DSS compliant
- Sensitive data is encrypted
- Regular security audits

### Data Protection
- Tokenization of payment information
- Fraud detection and prevention
- Secure data storage

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Verification failed?** Ensure all information matches your bank records
- **Payout delayed?** Check for holidays or bank processing times
- **Payment declined?** Verify sufficient funds and card details
