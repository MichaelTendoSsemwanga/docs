# Speaker Management with Loft

*Create an exceptional speaker experience while streamlining your event management. This guide covers the entire speaker lifecycle using Loft's platform features.*

## Quick Start

### Accessing Speaker Tools
1. Log in to your Loft account
2. Go to **Events** > **Your Event** > **Speakers**
3. Click **Manage Speakers**

### Key Features
- **Speaker Portals**: Dedicated dashboards
- **Content Management**: Upload and review materials
- **Communication**: Centralized messaging
- **Scheduling**: Session management
- **Analytics**: Speaker performance metrics

## Step 1: Speaker Recruitment

### 1.1 Create Speaker Applications
1. Go to **Speakers** > **Applications**
2. Click **+ New Application Form**
3. Customize fields:
   - Bio and headshot
   - Session details
   - Availability
   - Travel requirements

### 1.2 Share Call for Speakers
1. Copy the application link
2. Share via:
   - Email campaigns
   - Social media
   - Your event website
   - Speaker directories

## Step 2: Speaker Onboarding

### 2.1 Send Invitations
1. Select approved speakers
2. Click **Send Invitation**
3. Customize email template
4. Include:
   - Speaker portal login
   - Next steps
   - Key deadlines

### 2.2 Speaker Portal Setup
1. **Profile Completion**
   - Upload bio (500 words max)
   - Add headshot (800x800px, JPG/PNG)
   - Social media links
   - Speaker category

2. **Session Details**
   - Title and description
   - Format (keynote, panel, etc.)
   - Technical requirements
   - Co-presenter information

## Step 3: Content Management

### 3.1 Presentation Uploads
1. **Required Files**
   - Presentation slides (PDF/PPTX)
   - Handouts (PDF)
   - Video preview (MP4, 5 min max)

2. **Deadlines**
   - First draft: [Date]
   - Final version: [Date]
   - Last-minute updates: [Date]

### 3.2 Content Review
1. **Review Process**
   - Initial feedback within 72 hours
   - Two revision rounds included
   - Final approval required

2. **Technical Checks**
   - Format compliance
   - Branding guidelines
   - Accessibility standards

## Step 4: Speaker Communications

### 4.1 Automated Notifications
- Confirmation emails
- Reminders (7 days, 1 day before)
- Schedule updates
- Tech check reminders

### 4.2 Direct Messaging
1. **Inbox**
   - Centralized communication
   - Message templates
   - Read receipts

2. **Announcements**
   - Broadcast to all speakers
   - Targeted groups
   - Urgent alerts

## Step 5: Event Day

### 5.1 Speaker Check-in
1. **Onsite**
   - Dedicated speaker lounge
   - Tech support
   - Run-through schedule

2. **Virtual**
   - Green room access
   - Audio/video test
   - Moderation tools

### 5.2 Live Support
- Dedicated help desk
- Emergency contacts
- Backup presentations
- Session recording

## Post-Event

### 6.1 Thank You Package
1. **Digital Assets**
   - Session recording
   - Event photos
   - Social media kit

2. **Feedback**
   - Speaker survey
   - Testimonial request
   - Future speaking opportunities

### 6.2 Analytics Report
- Attendance numbers
- Engagement metrics
- Feedback scores
- Social media reach

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Speaker Can't Access Portal
1. Check email for invitation
2. Reset password if needed
3. Verify email domain whitelist

#### Missing Content
1. Check spam folder
2. Verify file size limits
3. Try alternative upload method

## Step 2: Recruit Speakers

### 2.1 Sourcing Strategies
1. **Open Call for Papers (CFP)**
   - Create submission form
   - Set clear guidelines
   - Promote across channels

2. **Direct Outreach**
   - Industry thought leaders
   - Past speakers
   - Partner organizations
   - Social media influencers

### 2.2 Speaker Application Form
**Required Fields**:
- Full name & title
- Company & role
- Bio (short and long versions)
- Headshot
- Session title & description
- Speaking experience
- Social media links

**Optional Fields**:
- Travel requirements
- Dietary restrictions
- Accessibility needs

## Step 3: Onboard Speakers

### 3.1 Speaker Welcome Kit
1. **Welcome Letter**
   - Thank you

## Step 4: Manage Content

### 4.1 Content Submission
- **Deadline**: 3-4 weeks before event
- **Required Materials**:
  - Final presentation (PPT/Keynote)
  - Speaker bio (100 words)
  - Headshot (high-res)
  - Social media handles
  - Additional resources

### 4.2 Content Review
1. **Initial Review** (1 week after submission)
   - Check branding
   - Verify content quality
   - Ensure no sales pitches

2. **Feedback & Revisions** (1 week)
   - Provide clear feedback
   - Set revision deadline
   - Final approval

## Step 5: Prepare for the Event

### 5.1 Technical Rehearsal
- **1-2 Weeks Before**:
  - Test A/V setup
  - Review slide transitions
  - Check microphone levels
  - Test virtual platform

### 5.2 Speaker Briefing
- **Session Flow**: Timing, Q&A, transitions
- **Room Setup**: Stage, seating, AV
- **Moderator**: Introduction, timekeeping
- **Contingencies**: Tech issues, time changes

## Step 6: Execute Flawlessly

### 6.1 Speaker Ready Room
- **Check-in Station**: Badges, materials
- **Tech Support**: Last-minute help
- **Green Room**: Refreshments, quiet space
- **Schedule Board**: Session updates

### 6.2 Session Management
- **15 Min Before**: Speaker arrives
- **5 Min Before**: Mic check
- **During**: Time signals, Q&A management
- **After**: Thank you, collect materials

### 7.1 Thank You Package

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Speaker Drops Out
1. **Prevention**
   - Have backup speakers
   - Collect materials in advance
   - Get signed agreements

2. **Solution**
   - Contact first backup
   - Update schedule
   - Notify attendees

#### Technical Difficulties
1. **Prevention**
   - Test all equipment
   - Have backup devices
   - Prepare offline options

2. **Solution**
   - Stay calm
   - Have IT support ready
   - Keep audience informed
