# Event Sponsorships & Partnerships

Attract and manage sponsors for your events with Loft's sponsorship management tools.

## Creating Sponsorship Packages

### Package Setup
1. Go to **Event Dashboard** > **Sponsors**
2. Click **Create Package**
3. Define package details:
   - **Package Name** (e.g., Gold, Silver, Bronze)
   - **Price** (or mark as custom)
   - **Quantity Available**
   - **Visibility** (public/private)

### Benefits & Inclusions
- Logo placement
- Speaking opportunities
- Booth space
- Social media mentions
- Email blasts
