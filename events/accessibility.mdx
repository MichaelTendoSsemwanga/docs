# Event Accessibility

Ensure your events are inclusive and accessible to all attendees with Loft's accessibility features.

## Venue Accessibility

### Physical Access
- **Wheelchair Access**: Ramps, elevators, wide doorways
- **Seating**: Accessible seating options
- **Restrooms**: ADA-compliant facilities
- **Parking**: Designated accessible spaces

### Wayfinding
- Clear signage
- Tactile paths
- High-contrast visuals
- Large print materials

## Digital Accessibility

### Website & Registration
- Screen reader compatibility
- Keyboard navigation
- Alt text for images
- Resizable text

### Virtual Events
- Closed captions
- Sign language interpretation
- Adjustable playback speeds
- Screen reader support

## Communication

### Before the Event
- Accessibility statement
- Detailed venue information
- Contact for special requests
- Pre-event materials in multiple formats

### During the Event
- Live captioning
- Sign language interpreters
- Assistive listening devices
- Quiet rooms

## Ticketing & Registration

### Accessible Tickets
- Companion tickets
- Personal assistant passes
- Early access options
- Custom ticket types

### Registration Process
- Clear instructions
- Multiple contact methods
- Special requirements field
- Flexible payment options

## Best Practices

### Planning
- Consult with accessibility experts
- Train staff and volunteers
- Test all access points
- Have backup plans

### During the Event
- Designate accessibility ambassadors
- Monitor access points
- Be prepared to make adjustments
- Collect feedback

- Captioning services

### Legal Requirements
- ADA compliance
- Local accessibility laws
- Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)
