# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the Loft platform.

## Account & Access

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### How do I change my account email?
Go to Account Settings > Email and follow the verification process.

## Event Management

### How do I duplicate an event?
Navigate to the event dashboard, click the three dots menu, and select "Duplicate Event."

### Can I change the event date after tickets have been sold?
Yes, but you should notify your attendees of the change through the messaging system.

## Ticketing

### How do I issue a refund?
Go to the order in your dashboard and select "Issue Refund." You can choose partial or full refunds.

### Can I transfer tickets between events?
No, tickets are specific to each event. You'll need to issue a refund and have the attendee purchase new tickets.

## Check-in & Scanning

### Does the check-in app work offline?
Yes, you can check in attendees without an internet connection. Data will sync when you're back online.

### What should I do if a ticket won't scan?
Try these steps:
1. Clean your device's camera lens
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Try manual check-in using the attendee's name or order number

## Payments & Payouts

### When will I receive my payout?
Payouts are typically processed 3-5 business days after your event ends.

### What payment methods can I accept?
We support all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers.

## Technical Support

### What are your system requirements?
- Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- Internet connection (3G/4G or WiFi)
- For the mobile app: iOS 13+ or Android 8.0+

### How do I report a bug?
Contact our support team with:
- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots if possible
- Browser/device information

## Contacting Support

For issues not covered in this FAQ, please [contact our support team](/troubleshooting/contact-support).
