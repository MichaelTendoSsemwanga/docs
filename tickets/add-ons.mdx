# Add-ons & Merchandise

Enhance your event offerings with additional products and services that attendees can purchase with their tickets.

## Creating Add-ons

### Basic Setup
1. Go to your event dashboard
2. Navigate to **Tickets** > **Add-ons**
3. Click **Create Add-on**
4. Enter the following details:
   - **Name**: Clear, descriptive title
   - **Description**: What's included
   - **Price**: Set price or mark as free
   - **Quantity Available**: Limit if needed
   - **Sales Period**: When it's available for purchase

### Types of Add-ons
- **Physical Items**: T-shirts, lanyards, merchandise
- **Digital Goods**: E-books, recordings, resources
- **Experiences**: Backstage passes, meet & greets
- **Donations**: Optional contributions

## Best Practices

### Product Presentation
- Use high-quality images
- Write clear descriptions
- Include dimensions/specs for physical items

### Pricing Strategy
- Price competitively
- Offer bundles for better value
- Consider cost-plus pricing

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Add-on not showing**: Check visibility settings
- **Inventory mismatch**: Reconcile with physical stock
- **Pricing errors**: Verify discount applications
